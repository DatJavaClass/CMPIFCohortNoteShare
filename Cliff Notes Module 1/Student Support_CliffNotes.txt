Student Support — Study Guide
Course: Introduction to Data-Centric Computing (CMPINF-2100)
Source: Student Support

================================================================
QUICK REFERENCE — WHERE TO GO BASED ON QUESTION TYPE
================================================================
  - Peer collaboration, non-urgent public Q       → Slack
  - Stuck on assignments / quizzes, non-urgent    → Discussion Forums
  - Live help, scheduled                          → Office Hours
  - Urgent or private matters                     → Email (ticket)

================================================================
1. SLACK
================================================================
  - Available throughout the entire MDS program
  - Purpose: peer connection and collaboration
  - For this course: join channel CMPINF2100
    (browse all channels OR search "CMPINF2100" and filter by
    channels)
  - Channels ARE monitored, but the teaching team does NOT
    respond there. For staff response → email.
  - Academic integrity rules apply — do NOT share answers or
    solutions on Slack.

================================================================
2. OFFICE HOURS
================================================================
  - Found under "Live Events" in Coursera
  - Also visible on the Coursera calendar
  - Automated email reminder ~24 hours before each session

================================================================
3. DISCUSSION FORUMS
================================================================
  - Located in Coursera, sorted by Module
  - Best for: non-urgent questions, getting unstuck on
    assignments/quizzes, general course questions
  - Teaching team monitors but DOES NOT GUARANTEE a response
  - Rules:
      - Do not post personal or sensitive information
      - Do not post large segments of your own completed code or
        answers
  - Urgent questions → email instead

================================================================
4. EMAIL — THE OFFICIAL CHANNEL
================================================================
  Address: online-mds-support@pitt.edu

  Use for:
    - Urgent questions
    - Anything requiring privacy
    - Anything that needs a guaranteed response

  Email-writing rules:
    - Include the course number "CMPINF 2100" in the email
    - Include the module or assignment number if applicable
    - This creates a support TICKET — that's how it gets routed
      to the right person

  WARNING: Emails sent to any other address may go unnoticed.
  Use this address unless specifically told otherwise.

================================================================
HIGH-VALUE PRACTICAL NOTES
================================================================
  - Only email provides a GUARANTEED response. Slack and forums
    are best-effort.
  - The course is online and large — support is structured
    around routing and tickets, not ad-hoc messaging.
  - Default mental model: peer help on Slack/forums; staff help
    via email; live help via office hours.
