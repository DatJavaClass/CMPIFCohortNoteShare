ACADEMIC BACKGROUND
================================================================

================================================================
TEACHING FOCUS
================================================================
Teaches data science to students across a wide range of
backgrounds — from those new to computational thinking to those
already deep in CS. Relevant to this course: it is explicitly
designed to accommodate both ends of that spectrum.

================================================================
RESEARCH AREAS
================================================================
Two main threads, both data-science-adjacent:

  - Natural Language Processing (NLP)
    Applying data science principles to large datasets of text
    or speech.

  - Computational Social Science
    Studying how people interact socially and form communities
    by analyzing logs of social behavior (typically online),
    using data science methods.

WHY THIS MATTERS FOR THE COURSE
================================================================
  - The instructor's research uses the same pipeline you'll
    learn. Examples drawn from class are likely to lean on text /
