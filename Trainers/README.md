# Trainers

## What these are

Trainers are per-subject documents designed to give insight and assistance on select topics, rather than covering a whole module.

Where the Cliff Notes and Cliff Jumper Notes follow the course module by module, a Trainer zooms in on a single subject and works through it in depth, the kind of thing you reach for when one topic in particular is giving you trouble.

## Currently available

- `Module-2_Python_Loops_Trainer.pdf`: a focused trainer on Python loops.
- `Module-4_Statistics_Simulation_Trainer.pdf`: a focused trainer on statistics and simulation.
- `Module-8-9_Data_Visualization_Toolkit_Trainer_Part_1.pdf`: a tools-focused trainer on the matplotlib, pandas, and seaborn functions behind the Module 8-9 plot types that the lectures and labs left you to look up on your own. Part 1 of 2; Part 2 begins at pair plots (Module 10).

## A note of caution

This is a study aid, not a textbook. Use it to understand and practice, but check anything important against the actual course material, especially before a quiz.
